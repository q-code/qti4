CSV/TXT files must include: role, login, password, email. Roles are: A (administrator), M (staff member), U (user). For the password you can use the sha1 key, the password with the prefix SHA, or let it empty to get the login as the password.<br /><b><i>Example of csv/txt file:</i></b><br />M; bill; 5753a498f025464d72e088a9d5d6e8725; bill@corp.com<br />
U; william; SHAwilly03; william@corp.com<br />
U; robert; ; robert@corp.com
